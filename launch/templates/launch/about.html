Welcome
to NoQueue

NoQueue is a web based application allowing customers to preorder their food for takeaway, as well as providing a centralized platform to order food across different restaurants.

NoQueue also presents an Artificial Intelligence based recommendation system which learns from the users' previous purchases and suggests different restaurants through previously assimilated data.
